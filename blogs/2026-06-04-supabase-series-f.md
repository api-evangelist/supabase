Supabase secured $500 million in Series F funding at a $10 billion pre-money valuation, led by GIC. The company is channeling investment toward accelerating open source development, including the launch of Multigres v0.1 alpha, a scalable operating system designed to address challenges with running Postgres at scale. With nearly 10 million developers now building on the platform and database launches growing 600% annually, Supabase is simultaneously investing in infrastructure reliability and expanded support systems.
